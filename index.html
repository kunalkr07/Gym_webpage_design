Weight training

Weight training is a type of strength training that uses weights to build muscle and increase strength. It can help improve your overall fitness, boost your metabolism, and reduce your risk of injury.

General Fitness

General fitness refers to the overall health and well-being of an individual. It involves a combination of regular exercise, proper nutrition, and healthy habits. Engaging in general fitness activities can help improve cardiovascular health, increase energy levels , and reduce the risk of chronic diseases.
